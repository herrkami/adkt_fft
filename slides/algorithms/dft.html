Matrixformulierung der DFT:

\( X_k \quad=\quad \sum\limits_{n=0}^{N-1} e^{-\frac{2\pi i}{N} nk} \cdot x_{n} \quad\stackrel{\omega = e^{-\frac{2\pi i}{N}}\\}{=}\quad \sum\limits_{n=0}^{N-1} \omega^{nk} \cdot x_{n} \)

\( \rightarrow X = W \cdot x = \begin{bmatrix} 1&1&1&1&\cdots &1 \\ 1&\omega&\omega^2&\omega^3&\cdots&\omega^{N-1} \\ 1&\omega^2&\omega^4&\omega^6&\cdots&\omega^{2(N-1)}\\ 1&\omega^3&\omega^6&\omega^9&\cdots&\omega^{3(N-1)}\\ \vdots&\vdots&\vdots&\vdots&\ddots&\vdots\\ 1&\omega^{N-1}&\omega^{2(N-1)}&\omega^{3(N-1)}&\cdots&\omega^{(N-1)(N-1)} \end{bmatrix} \cdot \begin{bmatrix} x_0\\ x_1\\ x_2\\ x_3\\ \vdots\\ x_{N-1} \end{bmatrix} \)
